About Me

I'm Shirley, SASC is my initial. thanks for browsing my page SASC.ONCEPT.
I'm graduated form the School of Design, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2008, major in Multimedia Design.
I'm working as Senior Multimedia Designer in my current position.
I'm specialized in prints, web, mobile application and showcase interface design.
I love watching movies, because through different movies,
I get inspired, I get taken to different depths and angles from different directors' eyes. Movies give me room to explore.


Work Enviorment
